Our Mission

CSSQ is an organization based in the DC Metro Area that wishes to inspire others to be better people and consider what they can do to help our environment. We as humans are the cause of climate change, but by working together, we can save our planet. It is never too late to find eco-friendly alternatives and actively cooperate to rebuilding our future.
