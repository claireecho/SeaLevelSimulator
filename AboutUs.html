About Us

After the loss of one of the triumvirate, the two founders were at a loss regarding what to do at HackTJ 10.0. They had planned for a team-up among friends, but were left with only each other. Thankfully, two newcomers had entered the scene and were willing to help make this idea a reality. Without their help, CSSQ would not be where it is today.

________

Our Mission

CSSQ is an organization based in the DC metro area that wishes to inspire others to be better people and consider what they can do to help our environment. We as humans are the cause of climate change, so by working together, we can save our planet. It is never too late to find eco-friendly alternatives and actively cooperate to rebuild our future.
